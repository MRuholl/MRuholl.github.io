Canada

Canada is the the largest country in land area in North America; and the second largest country in land area in the world. This country contains over 3.8 million square miles of land area.

Unlike a lot of coutries who have only one official language, Canada is unique in the fact that it has two offical languages: English and French. English is spoken throughout most of the territories. French speakers are mainly concentrated in the territoy of Quebec.
