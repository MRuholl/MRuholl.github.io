White Mushrooms 2 cartons
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon
Garlic 2 cloves
Cream Cheese 1 package (8 oz.)
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup
Ground Black Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Onion Powder 1/4 teaspoon
Ground Cayenne Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Clean mushrooms and remove stems . Do not throw stems away.
  3. Mince garlic cloves and mushroom stems.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add garlic and stems to the skillet and fry to reduce moisture. Remove from heat.
  6. Add cream cheese, Parmesan, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Stir together.
  7. Spoon out the mixture into the mushroom caps and place on a greased baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
